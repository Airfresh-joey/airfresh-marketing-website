SECTION 7: Clutch.co Review Strategy

⚠️ CRITICAL: Clutch rankings are primarily based on verified client reviews. Without reviews, your listing won't rank. Aim for 5+ reviews within the first month.

Review Requirements

  • Reviews must be from real clients (Clutch verifies)
  • Reviewers are contacted directly by Clutch for a 15-minute call
  • Reviews include project details, budget range, and ratings
  • Minimum 3 reviews to appear in search results

Ideal Review Candidates

Choose clients who:

  • Had a positive experience
  • Can speak to specific results
  • Are willing to spend 15 minutes on a verification call

SECTION 10: Additional Link Building Tactics

1. HARO (Help a Reporter Out)

What: Journalists post queries; you respond with expert quotes and get backlinks.
URL: helpareporter.com
Cost: Free
Time: 15-30 min/day

How to Use:

  1. Sign up at helpareporter.com as a "Source"
  2. Receive 3 emails daily with journalist queries
  3. Filter for marketing, events, business topics
  4. Respond quickly (within 2-3 hours) with expert quotes
  5. Include your credentials and company link

Topics to Monitor:

  • Marketing and advertising
  • Events and meetings
  • Small business
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Customer experience

2. Client/Partner Link Requests

Request backlinks from:

  • Clients' vendor/partner pages
  • Venue partner directories
  • Event sponsor acknowledgment pages
  • Industry association memberships

3. Broken Link Building

  1. Use Ahrefs or SEMrush to find broken links on event industry sites
  2. Create content that could replace the broken link
  3. Contact webmaster offering your content as replacement

4. Resource Page Link Building

Target resource pages on:

  • University event planning programs
  • Convention center vendor lists
  • Trade association resources
  • Event planning guides

5. Scholarship Link Building

Create a small scholarship ($500-$1000) for marketing students. Universities link to scholarship pages, providing .edu backlinks (highest value).

6. Data/Survey Link Building

Conduct original research/surveys and publish findings. Examples:

  • "State of Experiential Marketing 2026" report
  • "Brand Ambassador Industry Salary Survey"
  • "Event Marketing ROI Benchmarks"
