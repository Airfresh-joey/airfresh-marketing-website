Setup Steps
  • 1
    Open Box 3 (Spin Wheel) first. Handle carefully — this is the most fragile item. Remove gently, stand it upright. Do not force any part that doesn't move freely.
  • 2
    Secure the spin wheel base. Use zip ties (from Box 4) to stabilize the base if needed. We have a few extra — use them. A wobbly wheel is a broken wheel.
  • 3
    Unfold Wagons 1 and 2 (from Boxes 1 and 2). Unfold fully, lock wheels, and position them flanking the spin wheel or in a V formation toward foot traffic.
  • 4
    Check all branded artwork/graphics. Confirm all panels, wraps, or artwork are attached securely and display correctly. If anything looks off — photo it and text Kimberly immediately.
  • 5
    Load swag onto wagons. Distribute items evenly. Keep stock boxes nearby but out of sight. Tote bags are high volume — keep them stocked throughout the day.
  • 6
    Brief your team. 2-minute standup before activation goes live: talking points, no phone zones, swag limits per person, and spin wheel rotation duties.
  • 7
    Take a setup photo showing full activation from both sides. Send to Kimberly before 10:30 AM.

7
End-of-Day Breakdown
Non-Negotiable — Do This Every Night

Everything must be broken down, wrapped, and repacked every single night. Jhon picks up daily — nothing gets left on site. Nothing gets stacked carelessly. Every box gets saved and used. The artwork must be handled gently and stored safely.

  • 1
    Collect all remaining swag back into boxes. Do a rough count of what was distributed vs. what remains. Note it for Kimberly.
  • 2
    Fold and repack wagons into Boxes 1 and 2. Use zip ties to secure if wraps are detachable. Never throw branded wraps loosely into a box.
  • 3
    Artwork / branded panels: save every piece every night. Roll or flat-pack carefully. Do not fold in ways that crease the print. Use the original packaging if available.
  • 4
    Disassemble spin wheel carefully. Follow the reverse of setup. Do not force. Store back in Box 3, upright if possible. Re-wrap with original padding. Use tape from Box 4 to secure.
  • 5
    Repack Box 4 (vent kit). Everything that came in it should go back in — tape, scissors, zip ties, knife, extras.
  • 6
    Take breakdown photo of all 4 packed boxes before Jhon loads them. Send to Kimberly along with your daily recap (swag count, any issues, notes).
8
Spin Wheel — Fragile Handling Rules
Handle with Extreme Care
  • • Never lay the spin wheel flat. Always store and transport upright in Box 3.
  • • Never drop or let it tip over. Have a second person present when moving it.
  • • Use zip ties to stabilize the base during activation. Check tightness before each day starts.
  • • If anything feels loose or damaged — do not try to force it. Stop, take a photo, text Joey immediately.
  • • The artwork on the wheel is custom-printed. Protect it from scuffs, moisture, and direct contact with other surfaces during transport.
This is what correct assembly looks like — protect it.
Visual Reference

The wheel is red/white/blue with Cloudchipr QR codes and prize segments. Any scuff, crease, or damage to the printed surface is visible and permanent. Always handle from the base pole — never grab the wheel face directly.
